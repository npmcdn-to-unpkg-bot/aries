Project Concept

Most reliable touch experience available on a clear display. Precision control and almost full transparency. Must be front touch only.

We would use three of these: http://www.planar.com/products/transparent-displays set side by side to create a wide canvas. This wide canvas will be touch from front.

Detailed storyboards will need to be provided as well as all content assets needed. If requested CT digital can provide the storyboard development. Target rate for creative is ~$5k per week and projects usually require between 2 and 5 weeks depending on complexity.

The hardware testing, configuration, and system programming will require 4 weeks with a preferred 2-3 week planning phase to precede this.
